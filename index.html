Asif Alam

Researcher (RF & mmWave)

Asif Alam is a researcher specializing in RF and millimeter-wave technologies. He is a Presidential Scholar pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering at Florida International University (FIU). He is a member of the FIU Honors College and serves as a Student Ambassador for the IEEE Microwave Theory and Techniques Society (MTT-S). He has co-authored multiple papers and is an inventor on a patent. His research focuses on millimeter-wave antennas, substrate-integrated waveguide (SIW) technologies, and emerging RF design methodologies.

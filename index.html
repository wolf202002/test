Biography

Asif Alam is a Presidential Scholar pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering at Florida International University (FIU) in Miami, FL. He currently serves as the President of the Engineering Research Society and is a member of FIU’s Honors College. He has authored multiple publications and his first patent was granted recently. His research interests include millimeter-wave antennas and interconnects, flexible antennas, air-filled substrate-integrated waveguide (SIW) antennas, SIW technology, on-chip antennas, and emerging design techniques for millimeter-wave components. Beyond AFSIW and Antennas, he happens to be interested in financial markets and business.

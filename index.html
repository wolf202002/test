Asif Alam

Researcher (RF & mmWave)

Asif Alam is a Presidential Scholar pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering at Florida International University (FIU) in Miami, FL, USA. He is also a member of the Honors College at FIU and serves as a Student Ambassador for the IEEE Microwave Theory and Techniques Society (MTT-S). In 2023, he co-authored and presented a paper at the prestigious IEEE MTT-S International Microwave Symposium in San Diego, CA. His research interests include millimeter-wave antennas and interconnects, flexible antennas, air-filled substrate-integrated waveguide (SIW) antennas, SIW technology, on-chip antennas, and emerging design techniques for millimeter-wave components.
